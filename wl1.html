About Wilder Hulme

The Hulme Hall grounds have the potential to become a sanctuary for local wildlife. On behalf of the Hulme Hall Halls Exec committee, I have outlined possible actions we could take to improved biodiversity. I am appealing for support from the University as the project would benefit their living campus plan (as part of the Universityâ€™s Environmental Stainability Strategy). I also seek to work with local charities to help integrate our efforts with larger projects. I am proposing an initiative whereby making Hulme a home for Starlings (and other birds), Frogs, Bats, Wildflowers/Bees/insects, and hedgehogs would benefit residents. Improving their access to nature and providing them with volunteering opportunities. Our actions would fit into the universities Living Campus plan. I propose a stepwise approach to get easily implementable environmental projects approved by university estates. By getting residents engaged, the whole project will become one big event strengthening the community while creating something worthwhile.
